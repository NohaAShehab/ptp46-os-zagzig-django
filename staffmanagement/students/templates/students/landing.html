FAQ

Quick answers for common questions.

Is this landing page responsive?

Yes. It uses flexible grids and breakpoints so it looks good on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Do we need external CSS libraries?

No. The styling is self-contained inside `landing.html` so it works immediately.

Can I replace the placeholder text later?

Absolutely. Swap copy, buttons, and sections with your real product details or Django context variables.

How can we connect this to Django views?

Render this template from your `views.py` and pass context as needed. The HTML structure is ready.
